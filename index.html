The Problem

Tree service companies lose 30%+ of revenue on bad estimates. Crews spend 27 hours/week on unbillable on-site quotes that convert at only 40% — margin erosion from underbidding and lost deals from overbidding.

The Solution

AI estimating platform using satellite imagery + computer vision to measure canopy size, assess species risk, and generate accurate job estimates before anyone leaves the shop.

$680M
TAM
$70–100K

Break-even Customers

Competitive Moat Analysis: TreeMind AI benefits from a strong data network effect. Each job estimated improves the computer vision model's accuracy for canopy analysis, species identification, and risk assessment — creating a compounding advantage. Key condition: first mover who builds the training dataset from real tree jobs owns the moat.

Revenue Model: Starter ($199/mo) → Pro ($299/mo) → Enterprise ($499/mo). Annual contract discount 20%. LTV:CAC target ratio 4.1:1. Break-even at ~180 paying customers within 12–14 months.

Action Plan: Phase 1 — partner with 10 tree service companies to build training dataset. Phase 2 — launch satellite imagery estimation engine. Phase 3 — expand to crew scheduling and full operations platform.
